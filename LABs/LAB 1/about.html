About Us

String Alchemy

A passionate collective of artists and musicians united by our love for music and the magic of instruments. Our mission is to celebrate the beauty and craftsmanship of musical instruments through curated exhibitions, interactive workshops, and captivating performances. At String Alchemy, we believe in the transformative power of music to transcend boundaries and bring people together.
