Percy Jackson and the Olympians

Percy Jackson & the Olympians is a storm of adventure, mythology, and destiny—a saga where gods walk among mortals, heroes are forged in battle, and ancient prophecies shape the fate of the world. Percy Jackson, a seemingly ordinary kid, discovers he’s a demigod, thrown into a life where monsters lurk in the shadows and the gods of Olympus are anything but distant myths. Alongside his fierce allies, Annabeth and Grover, Percy embarks on quests that test his courage, loyalty, and the depths of his own powers. With humor as sharp as his sword and the sea as his ally, Percy stands against titans, traitors, and destiny itself—proving that sometimes, the greatest heroes are the ones who refuse to back down.

Percy Jackson

Percy Jackson isn't just a hero—he's a storm waiting to break, a tidal wave of sarcasm, loyalty, and raw power. Born to Poseidon but raised in the chaos of the mortal world, Percy is the demigod who never asked to be great but somehow always finds himself in the middle of saving Olympus. With the sea at his command and an unbreakable will, he faces gods, monsters, and prophecy-fueled disasters—all while cracking jokes and fiercely protecting those he loves. A natural leader with the heart of a warrior, Percy Jackson is a force of nature in sneakers.

Annabeth Chase

Annabeth Chase is the architect of dreams and the strategist of war, a daughter of Athena with an unshakable will and a sharp mind that cuts through even the trickiest puzzles. With storm-gray eyes full of wisdom beyond her years, she’s a warrior, a scholar, and a builder of worlds. Fiercely loyal and endlessly determined, Annabeth takes on monsters and gods alike with the confidence of someone who knows she’ll win—not because she’s the strongest, but because she’s the smartest. Whether she's leading a battle or sketching the foundation of a new Olympus, Annabeth is a force to be reckoned with.

Grover Underwood

Grover Underwood is the heartbeat of nature, a satyr with a soul as ancient as the forests and a loyalty that runs deeper than the roots of Yggdrasil. As Percy Jackson’s best friend and protector, Grover brings wisdom wrapped in nervous energy, humor tangled with courage. He may have hooves instead of sneakers, but when his friends are in danger, he charges in without hesitation. With a hunger for enchiladas and a dream to save the wild places of the world, Grover is a guardian of the earth, a seeker of lost demigods, and the very definition of a friend worth fighting for.

Luke Castellan

Luke Castellan is the storm before the war, a son of Hermes with charm as sharp as his dagger and a mind that spins plans like clockwork. He walks the line between hero and villain, torn by the betrayal of the gods and his own unwavering vision for change. A leader, a fighter, and a lost soul searching for justice, Luke is both a friend and a foe, driven by loyalty yet consumed by bitterness. He carries the weight of prophecy on his shoulders, and in the end, his story is one of sacrifice, redemption, and the battle between destiny and free will.

The Heroes of Olympus

The Heroes of Olympus is a clash of worlds, a saga where Greek and Roman demigods must unite against an ancient force rising from the depths of the earth. Bound by prophecy, seven heroes—Percy, Annabeth, Jason, Piper, Leo, Frank, and Hazel—embark on a journey that spans continents, battling giants, unraveling mysteries, and confronting their own destinies. As the Earth goddess Gaea awakens, threatening to consume the world, alliances are tested, friendships forged in fire, and sacrifices made in the name of survival. With war looming and the gods divided, these heroes must bridge the gap between two legacies and prove that unity is stronger than fate itself.

Jason Grace

Jason Grace is lightning bottled in human form—a son of Jupiter with the weight of the sky on his shoulders and the unshakable heart of a leader. Destined for greatness yet haunted by the expectations of gods and mortals alike, he stands as a beacon of justice, wielding storms as both a weapon and a shield. With a mind sharpened by strategy and a soul bound by duty, Jason faces the impossible with quiet resolve, commanding respect with every battle he fights and every sacrifice he makes. He’s the kind of hero legends whisper about—one who rises not because he wants to, but because he must.

Piper McLean

Piper McLean is wildfire wrapped in charm—a daughter of Aphrodite who defies expectations at every turn. Fiercely independent and unafraid to speak her mind, she wields charmspeak like a blade, bending words to her will with a power that can sway gods and mortals alike. But beneath the rebellious streak and effortless confidence lies a heart that fights for love, loyalty, and the people who matter most. Whether she’s leading a battle or standing up for what’s right, Piper is proof that beauty is more than skin deep—it’s strength, courage, and the fire to change the world.

Frank Zhang

Frank Zhang is quiet strength wrapped in uncertainty—a son of Mars with the heart of a warrior and the soul of a protector. He carries the weight of his family's legacy, gifted with the rare ability to shape-shift into any creature, yet burdened by the fragility of his own fate. Despite his doubts, Frank rises to every challenge, proving that true power isn’t just in brute force, but in kindness, loyalty, and the courage to lead when the world needs him most. Whether wielding a bow or commanding an army, Frank is a hero who burns bright, even when the odds are against him.

Hazel Levesque

Hazel Levesque is a warrior forged by time—a daughter of Pluto who has walked the line between life and death and emerged stronger for it. With gold-flecked eyes that see beyond the surface, she carries the weight of a past she never asked for and a future she’s determined to shape herself. Gifted with the power to summon precious metals from the earth, yet cursed by their dark consequences, Hazel fights not just for victory, but for redemption. Whether taming the immortal horse Arion or standing against impossible odds, she proves that true strength isn’t just in power—it’s in resilience, heart, and the courage to rewrite fate.

Reyna Ramirez Arellano

Reyna Ramírez-Arellano is strength forged in battle—a daughter of Bellona who carries the weight of leadership with unwavering resolve. A warrior, a strategist, and a protector, she commands respect with every step, leading not through fear, but through sheer determination and honor. With obsidian-black eyes that see through deception and a heart that refuses to break, Reyna stands as a pillar of loyalty and sacrifice. Whether guiding Camp Jupiter or forging alliances between worlds, she proves that true power isn’t just in might—it’s in wisdom, resilience, and the courage to stand alone when necessary.

Leo Valdez

Leo Valdez is fire and mischief wrapped in a mechanic’s jumpsuit—a son of Hephaestus with a mind that sparks like the machines he builds. A genius inventor with a heart too big for his own good, Leo hides his pain behind jokes, but when the world needs saving, he’s the one who turns the impossible into reality. With flames at his fingertips and a tool belt full of tricks, he’s the guy who can fix anything—except maybe his own destiny. Whether piloting the Argo II or cracking jokes in the face of danger, Leo proves that heroes aren’t just born—they’re built, one crazy idea at a time.

Nico Di Angelo

Nico di Angelo is shadows and silence woven into a warrior—a son of Hades who walks the line between the living and the dead with an unshakable resolve. Known as the Ghost King, he commands the underworld with a power that makes even the gods wary. Haunted by loss and burdened by prophecy, Nico is a force of nature, wielding Stygian iron and necromancy with effortless precision. Though he often stands alone, his loyalty runs deep, and his quiet strength proves that even the darkest souls can shine.
