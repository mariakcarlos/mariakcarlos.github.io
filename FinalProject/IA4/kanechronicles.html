The Kane Chronicles

The Kane Chronicles is a whirlwind of magic, mythology, and destiny—a saga where ancient Egyptian gods walk among mortals, and two siblings must rise to meet their fate. Carter and Sadie Kane, descendants of powerful pharaohs, are thrust into a world of chaos when their father’s experiment goes horribly wrong, unleashing the god Set. As they uncover their family’s deep connection to Egyptian magic, they must master their abilities, navigate treacherous alliances, and battle forces that threaten to plunge the world into darkness. With gods, monsters, and ancient prophecies shaping their journey, Carter and Sadie prove that true power lies not just in magic, but in courage, loyalty, and the strength to rewrite history.

Sadie Kane

Sadie Kane is chaos wrapped in confidence—a daughter of Egyptian magic who bends the rules as easily as she bends reality. Fiercely independent and endlessly witty, she wields spells with the ease of a seasoned magician, yet never loses her rebellious streak. With caramel-colored hair streaked in wild colors and a sharp tongue that keeps gods and mortals alike on their toes, Sadie is a force of nature. Whether facing ancient deities or navigating the complexities of fate, she proves that power isn’t just in magic—it’s in defiance, heart, and the courage to carve her own path.

Carter Kane

Carter Kane is quiet strength wrapped in ancient magic—a son of Egyptology who carries the weight of history and destiny on his shoulders. Disciplined, intelligent, and fiercely protective, he follows the path of Horus, wielding magic with precision and leading with unwavering resolve. Though he spent years traveling the world with his father, Carter never truly felt at home—until he discovered his place in the battle between gods and mortals. With a warrior’s heart and a scholar’s mind, he proves that true power isn’t just in spells or weapons—it’s in wisdom, loyalty, and the courage to stand against chaos.

Zia Rashid

Zia Rashid is fire and precision wrapped in ancient magic—a magician of the House of Life with a fierce will and a mastery over the elements. Raised in the First Nome after tragedy wiped out her village, she became one of the most skilled magicians of her generation, wielding flames with effortless control and standing as a formidable force against chaos. Though duty and discipline define her, Zia is more than just a warrior—she is a survivor, a strategist, and a protector of those she trusts. Whether facing gods or unraveling mysteries, she proves that true strength isn’t just in power—it’s in resilience, wisdom, and the courage to forge her own path.

Walt Stone

Walt Stone is magic and mortality intertwined—a magician of the House of Life cursed by his ancestry yet determined to defy fate. A skilled sau, or charm maker, Walt carries the bloodline of Akhenaton, a pharaoh whose defiance of the gods left his descendants doomed to die young. Despite the looming shadow of his curse, Walt fights alongside Carter and Sadie Kane, wielding enchanted amulets and ancient spells with effortless skill. His journey takes an unexpected turn when he becomes the human host for Anubis, the god of death, merging their souls to survive. With wisdom beyond his years and a quiet strength that refuses to fade, Walt proves that destiny is not just something to accept—it’s something to challenge.

Anubis

Anubis is the shadow between life and death—a god of funerals who walks unseen, guiding souls to their final judgment. Unlike his mythological counterpart, he takes on a more personal role in the series, appearing as a mysterious, dark-eyed teenager who catches Sadie Kane’s attention. Though bound by his divine duties, Anubis struggles with his connection to the mortal world, especially as his relationship with Sadie deepens. Eventually, he merges with Walt Stone, allowing him to experience life in a way no god has before. With wisdom beyond his years and a presence that lingers like a whisper from the underworld, Anubis proves that even death itself can evolve.

Bast

Bast is elegance wrapped in ferocity—a goddess of felines who moves with the grace of a predator and the loyalty of a guardian. Once the Eye of Ra, she fought against chaos incarnate, battling Apophis in the Duat for centuries. When freed, she became the protector of Carter and Sadie Kane, guiding them through the dangers of Egyptian magic with sharp instincts and sharper claws. Though she thrives in battle, Bast is also playful, mischievous, and fiercely devoted to those she calls family. Whether facing monsters or lounging in the sun, she proves that true strength isn’t just in power—it’s in resilience, cunning, and the heart of a warrior
